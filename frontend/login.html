About intelliQ


Intelliq hosts "intelligent" surveys and questionnaires with custom branching question paths for each respondent, depending on his answers.
The survey participants can log in and submit their answers, either anonymously or not.
Graphs and stats are presented to the admin for each question and new questionnaires can be uploaded as json files.

Developed for the module "Software Engineering" at National Technical University of Athens by
Raptopoulos Petros, Kogios Dimitrios, Chatzis John, Safos Konstantinos, Baris George, Kountourakis Minos


The code is uploaded to Github: https://github.com/ntua/SoftEng22-24
